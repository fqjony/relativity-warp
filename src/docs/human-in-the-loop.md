# Human-in-the-Loop Control

## The tension

Automation wants speed. Humans want safety. The answer is not removing people, but putting them at the right checkpoints.

## Where humans should decide

- When scope expands.
- When risks are elevated.
- When outputs are ambiguous.

## How to keep it efficient

- Provide concise artifacts, not long explanations.
- Show diffs and outputs in a reviewable format.
- Use default paths and known destinations.

## The outcome

Humans stay in control, while automation handles the mechanical work.
